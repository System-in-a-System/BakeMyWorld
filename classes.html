Baking Classes

As one famous Greek philosopher once said "Happiness is a piece of cake" and our bakery classes are a great training of its mastery.

Whether you are in a group or going solo, trying to make a cake (and failing) is a great way to spend a Sunday morning or afternoon.

Our talented Head Chefs pass on their experience of working in one of London's most popular bakeries, and show you how to ice cupcakes with the Primrose signature swirl. You will be shown how to ice using a palette knife, create masterpieces with piping and best of all, you get to take home all you make in the class!
